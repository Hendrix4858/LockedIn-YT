About LockedIn

LockedIn is an open-source browser extension designed to help you take back control of your YouTube experience. It provides customizable toggles to hide distracting elements like Shorts, recommendations, comments, thumbnails and more. Users can enable or disable features with a single click from the extension popup, allowing for a distraction-free, intentional YouTube browsing experience. The extension works entirely locally with zero data collection, ensuring your privacy is always protected. Whether you're using YouTube for learning, research, or entertainment, LockedIn helps you stay focused on what matters by blocking algorithmic distractions and keeping you in control of your viewing habits.
